about the makerspace

Founded in 2013, the Enjigo Foundation supports education in engineering, science, technology and the arts, and operates spaces for learning, teaching, and collaborative making.

We welcome you to come to our weekly meetings, Wednesday, 7:00 PM at our makerspace in downtown Salt Lake City. We’re in the Wright Building at 30 East Kensington (1500 South), SLC —see our Google Map.

MakeSLC Yardsale

by Duane Johnson - Mar 18th, 2013 - posted in Events | Comments

We’re having a Maker Yard Sale fundraiser! On Wednesday Mar 27th, we invite you to bring any “junk” you have from home that might be useful to someone else, and sell it at our community yard sale.

You can also bring a “skill” to be auctioned off at the event–if you’re a potter, or an electronics guy, or a software gal, or if you know a thing or two about 3D printers, for example, you could offer your expertise to fellow makers. Contact canadaduane@gmail.com if you’re willing to mentor someone for an evening and help fund the makerspace.

All proceeds will go toward buying a liability insurance policy–in other words, we’ll be able to start stocking the space with probably-not-too-dangerous but still-needs-a-liability-policy type equipment like laser cutters and milling machines!
